Our Women
on the Ground

Zahra Hankir

"Our Women On The Ground" is a collection of essays by Arab women reporting from the Arab world edited by Zahra Hankir. The book does not shy away from the front lines and splashes copious amounts of reality onto readers who dare to venture into its chapters. It is an emotional collection of essays that offers a small insight into the lives of several Arab women who are journalists and photo-journalists. The women in this collection range in backgrounds and experiences, which serve to offer a multitude of views on their experiences
